Hey there, welcome to mochi.

I suppose you're here to shorten your url? to do that, paste this into the url bar of your browser after editing the placeholders

{{base_url}}/?url=the_url_you_wanna_shorten

want an example? {{base_url}}/?url=https://example.com

That's it, as simple as that. However, we have a lot other features, to learn about them refer to the README of https://github.com/synbhwl/mochi
